The Steps

Haim

Over a twangy classic rock groove Daniella Haim’s rich voice drips with frustration and disappointment as she coos to her baby, “I can’t understand why / you don’t understand me!” The lyrics are both simple and gut wrenching, and the groovy guitar riffs would make Fleetwood Mac proud. Haim’s latest single is a song about trying your best and still disappointing those around you.

Frequent collaborator Rostam Batmanglij is back, most presently in the bridge where he pans the vocals back and forth across the stereo spectrum. “If I go right / And you go left / Hey, I know we'll meet up again.” Otherwise this track sticks to Haim’s winning formula of low-production pop-rock. With a simple drum line and some infectious strumming, the trio of sisters voices their dismay in a feel-good anthem for anyone who is just trying their best. “Every time I think that I am taking the steps / you end up at mad at me for making a mess.”
